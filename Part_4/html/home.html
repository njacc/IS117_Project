Home



A simple object viewer for very primitive forms. Please be aware, this is primarily for desktop users and screensizes.

  • Click and hold the viewport to rotate the camera
  • Hold right click in the viewporto move the camera
  • Zoom in and out in the viewport to move forward or backward
  • Select objects by clicking on them in the viewport
  • Change object properties via the property tab

  • Languages: HTML5, CSS, Javascript
  • ThreeJS Library (Ver. 0.161.0)
  • Bootstrap v5.0.2
